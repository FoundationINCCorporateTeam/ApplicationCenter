Frequently Asked Questions

Can I switch plans at any time?

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and we'll prorate any billing adjustments.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and for Enterprise customers, we can arrange invoicing.

Is there a free trial?

Yes! The Pro plan comes with a 7-day free trial. Credit card is required to start. You'll have full access to all Pro features during the trial.

What happens if I exceed my member limit?

We'll notify you when you're approaching your limit. You can upgrade to a higher plan or remove inactive members. Unless enabled, we won’t automatically charge you for overages; however, if you exceed the allowed limit, your plan will become inactive.

Do you offer pay-as-you-go plans?

Yes! We offer pay-as-you-go plans for all users. These are used to enable specific features or customize service availability to your liking.
